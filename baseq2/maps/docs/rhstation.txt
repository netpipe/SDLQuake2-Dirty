* Play Information *
Single Player           : Not intended (only for sightseeing)
Cooperative             : No
Deathmatch              : Not intended (weapons? which weapons?)
CTF                     : No 
Deathmatch (AQ2)        : Yes
Teamplay (AQ2)		: Yes - Starting points are not line of sight
* Construction *
Base                    : From scratch, idea from the station in
                          Euskirchen, Germany (Eifel)
Editors used            : Qoole V2.50 (If they only used integer
                            for their calculations...)
                          Emacs (to correct positions because of
                            wrong rounded floating point values
                            from qoole and for the texture disaster
                            - you know, the copyright thing)
                          Wally V1.24B for the textures.
Known Bugs              : When you are standing on the far of side
                          near the wall (near the waiting room) and
                          look to the buildings side door, some inner
                          brushes disappear (I wasn't able to correct
                          this, any ideas?). No other known bugs.
